Our Experience

Sector Experience

Understanding the drivers, dynamics and particular challenges of a sector is critical to how we operate. Our people have interacted with multiple industries.

We understand the seasonal nature, regulatory requirements and stakeholder complexity of tourism and hospitality. Our team has worked with operators, lenders and owners across accommodation, attractions and events to deliver practical outcomes in restructuring and insolvency matters.

Agricultural and rural businesses face unique challenges including commodity cycles, weather events and succession planning. We have extensive experience advising farmers, agribusinesses and rural service providers through financial distress and restructuring.

Dealerships, distributors and automotive supply chains require specialist knowledge of inventory, floor plan finance and manufacturer relationships. Our experience in this sector helps stakeholders navigate insolvency and restructuring with minimal disruption.

We work with banks, non-bank lenders and other financial institutions as secured creditors, appointors and advisors. Our team understands regulatory expectations, security structures and the need for clear communication with borrowers and other stakeholders.

Manufacturing businesses often have significant fixed assets, supply chain dependencies and workforce considerations. We have experience across a range of manufacturing subsectors, helping stakeholders preserve value and manage operational and financial restructuring.

Logistics, freight and passenger transport involve complex regulatory and safety frameworks. Our team has advised on insolvency and restructuring in road, rail and related sectors, with a focus on continuity of operations and asset realisation.

Education providers face specific challenges including regulatory compliance, funding models and student welfare. We have experience working with training organisations, private colleges and related entities through financial difficulty and restructuring processes.

We approach matters involving Indigenous organisations and communities with cultural awareness and respect for community governance. Our experience includes advising on restructuring and insolvency in a way that considers social impact and stakeholder expectations.
