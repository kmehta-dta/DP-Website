Our Services

Restructuring & Turnaround

We work with businesses to identify the causes of underperformance and develop strategies to improve profitability and cash flow. Our goal is to save businesses and preserve value for stakeholders.

Corporate Insolvency

When a company faces insolvency, we provide expert advice and formal appointment services, including Voluntary Administrations, Liquidations, and Receiverships.

Personal Insolvency

We assist individuals facing unmanageable debt through formal and informal arrangements, including Bankruptcy and Personal Insolvency Agreements (Part X).

Forensic Investigation

Reveal the facts. DuncanPowell has deep experience in conducting financial investigations. We also work with our digital forensic partner, Digital Trace Australia, to provide a comprehensive service to clients, including the application of e-discovery tools.
